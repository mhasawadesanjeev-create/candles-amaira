Our Story

Bringing Elegance and Glow to Your Heart

Welcome to Candles by Amaira – where a mother's creativity meets a daughter's wonder, creating magic one candle at a time.

Our journey began in our home kitchen, with 5-year-old Amaira as my enthusiastic little assistant. What started as a creative project has blossomed into a labor of love that we share with you. Every candle tells our story – of giggles during the pouring process, careful fragrance selections, and the pure joy of creating something beautiful together.

We believe in keeping things natural and simple. That's why we use only premium soy wax and beeswax, blended with pure essential oils to create fragrances that are as safe as they are delightful. No synthetic chemicals, no artificial colors – just honest, handcrafted candles made with ingredients we trust in our own home. Whether you're looking for everyday candles to brighten your home, or customized designs for birthdays, pujas, weddings, and special celebrations, we're here to bring your vision to life. We welcome bulk orders for events and parties, and offer a variety of products at different price points to suit every budget – because everyone deserves the warmth of a beautiful, natural candle.

Each candle is hand-poured with love. From our family to yours, we hope our candles bring warmth, comfort, and a little bit of magic to your special moments
